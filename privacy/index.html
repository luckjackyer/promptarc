Privacy Policy

This policy explains how PromptArc handles information related to the prompt library, image generation workspace, downloads, and contact requests.

Information we collect

PromptArc may collect information you submit directly, such as an email address for downloads or support, plus basic usage information such as pages visited, referral sources, and generation history stored in your browser or account workflow.

How information is used

Information is used to deliver requested downloads, respond to support messages, improve prompt examples, maintain generation history, prevent abuse, and understand which pages are useful to visitors.

Third-party services

This site may link to third-party tools, product partners, and digital product platforms. Their own privacy policies apply to any data collected on their sites.

Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to support@promptarc.cc.
