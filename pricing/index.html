Questions before paying

Know the credit rules before you spend.

Can I test image quality before paying?

Yes. Start with Free credits in the generator so you can inspect quality, ratio, and prompt direction first.

Does a failed generation consume credits?

Failed generations are reviewed during private rollout. The public rule will be shown before paid checkout opens.

Do credits expire?

Expiration terms will be published before public checkout. Do not buy credits until those rules are clear to you.

What happens after payment opens?

You will be able to connect credits to your account, generate images, and review usage from member history.

Can I get a refund or invoice?

Refund and invoice rules will be visible before any paid credit purchase is available.

Why is credit purchase in private rollout?

The generator is collecting real demand and failure data before public credit packs and Monthly credit plans open.
