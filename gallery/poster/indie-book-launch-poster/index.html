Book Launch poster prompt

Use this Book Launch poster prompt when you need event posters, campaign artwork, editorial one-pagers, and print-style visual systems. The theme on this example points to book and launch.

booklaunch

Prompt

Create a refined indie book launch poster with a dramatic unbranded book object, paper texture, soft editorial lighting, literary atmosphere, and clear title-safe negative space. No author photos, no publisher logos, no real book covers.
