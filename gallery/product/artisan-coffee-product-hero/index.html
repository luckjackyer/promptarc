Coffee product prompt

Use this Coffee product prompt when you need premium ecommerce product shots, launch creatives, and conversion-focused commercial visuals. The theme on this example points to coffee.

coffee

Prompt

Create a premium ecommerce hero image for an artisan coffee bag on a warm stone surface. Use morning side light, restrained coffee bean props, soft shadows, clean negative space for headline text, realistic paper texture, and a refined specialty-coffee brand mood. Avoid visible brand logos, readable text, extra products, and cluttered cafe backgrounds.
