Show a central desk, monitor, chair, keyboard, lamp, posture guide, and modular icon callouts for monitor height, chair support, arm position, lighting, and break rhythm. Use a clean educational layout with readable hierarchy, soft neutral colors, and no dense microcopy or brand logos.
