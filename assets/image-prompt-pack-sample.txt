PromptArc Image Prompt Pack - Free Sample

Use these prompts as reusable starting points. Replace the bracketed details with your own product, audience, brand style, or visual direction.

1. Product Ad Hero
Create a premium product advertising image for [product]. Place the product as the hero object on a clean reflective surface, use soft directional lighting, realistic shadows, subtle background props, and generous negative space for headline copy. The image should feel like a polished ecommerce campaign, not a generic stock photo.

2. SaaS UI Hero
Create a modern SaaS landing page hero mockup for [product category]. Include a clear headline area, dashboard preview, social proof strip, primary CTA, soft gradient background, and crisp spacing. Make the design feel conversion-focused and production-ready.

3. Event Poster
Design a bold event poster for [event name]. Use strong typography hierarchy, one memorable central visual, dynamic color blocking, and enough spacing for date, location, and CTA details. Make it print-ready and readable from a distance.

4. Brand Mascot
Create an original friendly mascot for [brand type]. Use a simple recognizable silhouette, warm expression, polished 3D illustration style, soft studio lighting, and a neutral background suitable for a startup brand kit.

5. Prompt QA Checklist
- Is the subject specific?
- Is the composition described?
- Is the lighting direction clear?
- Is the output use case named?
- Did you avoid copyrighted character or celebrity references?
- Did you leave text-safe space if the image is for an ad or poster?
