SEO Content Brief Prompt Template

Use this prompt to generate a content brief around a target keyword, its search intent, supporting questions, and a practical conversion angle.

Prompt template

Create an SEO content brief for the keyword: [keyword]

Audience: [target audience]
Business goal: [email capture, affiliate click, product sale, awareness]
Site type: [tool site, SaaS, content site, marketplace]

Return:
1. Search intent
2. Recommended article angle
3. Proposed title ideas
4. Section-by-section outline
5. FAQ questions to include
6. Internal link opportunities
7. Conversion CTA suggestion

Keep the brief practical and avoid generic filler sections.

Why it works

This format forces the model to think beyond search volume and toward intent plus conversion. That matters more for a small site than pumping out generic top-of-funnel content.
