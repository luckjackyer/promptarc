Cold Email Personalization Prompt

This prompt helps turn basic prospect data into short personalization angles for outbound messages without producing fake flattery or bloated intros.

Prompt template

You are helping personalize a cold email.

Prospect role: [role]
Company: [company]
What the company does: [summary]
Relevant trigger: [launch, hiring, funding, job post, content, product update]
Offer: [what you sell]

Generate:
1. Three personalized opening angles
2. One subject line for each angle
3. One short email intro for the best angle

Rules:
- Be concise
- Use only the information provided
- Avoid fake compliments
- Make the angle relevant to the offer

Important caution

Do not let the model invent facts. If you are pulling trigger data manually, keep the context block tight so the output stays grounded.
