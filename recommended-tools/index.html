Monetization research

Do not sell a prompt pack too early.

Before PromptArc has live image generation or a marketplace, the main product should be a categorized prompt-and-image gallery. Monetization stays experimental.

01

SEO traffic first

Build long-tail category pages around concrete searches such as product ad prompts, poster prompts, UI mockup prompts, and portrait prompts.

02

Affiliate links later

Only add tool recommendations where they match intent. Do not crowd the gallery with ads before traffic and click data exist.

03

Sponsored placements

Once prompt categories rank, test one tasteful sponsor slot per category page instead of many banners.

04

Real product later

A paid product makes more sense after live image generation, saved collections, user submissions, or a prompt marketplace exists.
